Over time, I have acquired the technical depth to operate across the **full ML stack** — but the drive has always been the same: make computers solve harder problems, more reliably, at greater scale.

*   **Security & Compliance:** Clinical PII sanitization at **0.87+ F1 scores**. RBAC enforcement and Adversarial Attack defense.
*   **Execution Philosophy:** Defaulting to Causal Inference and classical ensembles when DL is wasteful. Mentoring ML Guilds and authoring foundational RFCs.
